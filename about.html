Why DJ?

The COVID-19 pandemic changed lives in countless ways, and for me, it became the unexpected reason I started to learn DJing. Before the outbreak, I was just a regular student, busy with daily routines, social gatherings, and my studies. But when lockdowns were imposed and the world seemed to shut down, I found myself with more time than I knew what to do with. This free time, combined with the need for a creative outlet, pushed me to dive into something I had always been curious about: becoming a DJ.

Why Computer Science?

Computer science offers endless possibilities for innovation. I recognize how vital technology is in today's world, with industries relying on it for advancement. By studying computer science, I am preparing myself to contribute to this fast-evolving field, knowing that I can make a meaningful impact through the skills I acquire.
